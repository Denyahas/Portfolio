About me.

About Me.

My name is Deleu Denis, I am 22 years old. I was born in Moldova but moved to Russia when I was one year old. I didn’t attend kindergarten, and at eight, I started first grade. However, in the middle of the school year, we had to move back to Moldova due to family circumstances, where I completed my schooling. At 15 or 16, I was introduced to programming, specifically the C++ language. At the time, it seemed incredibly difficult, but I kept learning. However, at 16, I had to move back to Russia, where I worked part-time at a car wash and didn’t have the opportunity to pursue programming. Until the age of 22, I worked in various jobs, such as a barista, waiter, and others. I searched for my path, trying new things and exploring different fields. Each time, I found myself returning to programming and realized it was truly what I needed. So, I decided to pursue it as seriously as possible.

Programming and Design.

I really love programming and spend time learning it almost every day, always aiming to be better than I was yesterday. I can see my skills improving, and I’m not planning to stop. I’m also working on developing my design skills, as I believe design is an important part of life. We encounter design everywhere, every day. My style might not appeal to everyone, but I’m still learning, and everyone has different tastes - so I appreciate your understanding.

Hobbies.

When I’m not programming, I enjoy playing video games. I get more pleasure from online games than offline ones, so feel free to invite me to play League of Legends (D◡D). In the evenings, I like to watch movies and series with my wife. I also love going for walks, either alone or with someone. When I walk alone, I enjoy listening to music.
